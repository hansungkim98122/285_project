
Below are the constrains that you must follow when constructing the array.
(1) The change of consecutive altitude should be smaller than 2 and greater than -2
(2) The terrain should be smooth and continuous.
(3) Start the terrain with 0.1.

Your designed array should be wrapped with "[]", and each number should be separated with ",".

Some helpful tips for designing a terrain :
(1) You do not have to utilize all the numbers in the range.
(2) It should resemble a real-world ground in terms of roughness and non-linearility.
